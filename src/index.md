## About the project

Welcome to the research project website for the **Electron Microscopy Surface Defect Research** group at **King's College London**.

Our project uses advanced electron microscopy techniques to identify and characterise surface defects in engineering components. We work with academic and industrial partners to improve the reliability and safety of critical engineering materials.

Surface defects in engineering components — such as cracks, pits, and inclusions — can compromise structural integrity and lead to premature failure. Our research applies high-resolution electron microscopy to detect, classify, and analyse these defects at the micro and nanoscale, providing insights that support quality control, failure analysis, and materials development.

We are based in the Department of Engineering at King's College London and collaborate with partners across academia and industry.
